Pelé The King

Tribute to one of greatest footballer ever seen in the World

Here is a time line about The King

  • 1940 - Born in Três Corações-MG Brazil
  • 1945 - His family change to Baurú-SP, time that he got the nickname "Pelé" because he was fan of Vasco goalkeeper Pilé
  • 1950 - Time that Brazil is defeated for Uruguay in Maracanã and then Pelé promises to his father that he will win the World Cup
  • 1951 - For the first time Pelé wears a football boots playing for Ameriquinha where he wins the Infant Title of Baurú
  • 1954 - The manager Waldemar de Brito from Baurú AC invites Pelé to play for the club, until 16 years old he remains on juvenile
  • 1956 - August 6th Pelé signs your contract with Santos, where he remains until October 1974
  • 1957 - His debut for Brazil International Team against Argentina, he scores your first goal for Brazil and he did your first game on Maracanã
  • 1958 - March 31st the manager Feola called up Pelé for Brazil National Team as he playing the World Cup for the first time
  • 1966 - February got married Rosemeri dos Reis, he got three children with her: Kelly Cristina, Edinho and Jennifer. Brazil have been eliminated on the group stage of World Cup 1966 in England
  • 1970 - Alongside Gerson,Tostão and Jairzinho Pelé conquests triple World Cup for Brazil in Mexico
  • 1972 - Against Universidad from Mexico in San Francisco, California Pelé scores your Thousandth goal as he playing for Santos
  • 1974 - Second half 22 minutes Santos x Ponte Preta, October 2nd Pelé bow down on the pitch, he turns to four side of the pitch and says good bye to Santos
  • 1975 - Pelé go to White House to find USA's President for the first time. He signs record contract for 5 millions dollars with New york Cosmos
  • 1977 - Pelé says good bye to football in the game between Santos and Cosmos. He plays the first half for brazilian club and then the second half for americans, he scores a goal
  • 1981 - He's elected Athlete of the Century by french newspaper L'Equipe
  • 1994 - Ambassador of football in USA he's the feature in the World Cup
  • 1995 - He is sworn in sports minister by Fernando Henrique Cardoso
  • 2014 - Pelé got a surgery in the spine, he remains hospitalized on Albert Einstein Hospital
  • 2016 - Pelé is elected greatest athlete ever
  • 2022 - Pelé died at 3:27 pm December 29th, aged 82
